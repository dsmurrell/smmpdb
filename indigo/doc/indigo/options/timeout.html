Timeout options¶

timeout = 0¶

type:integer
default:0
description:Timeout (is ms) for Indigo operation. Zero means no timeout.

aam-timeout = 0¶

type:integer
default:0
description:Timeout (is ms) for atom-to-atom mapping computation operation. Zero means no timeout.
